Jordan Devos

Talk: Designing for Readability – A Guide to Web Typography
Time：10 AM - 12 PM, February 1st, 2021

Description: Traditionally, graphic designers have always studied the fundamentals of typography in terms of print design. But as the role of the designer now spans across all types of digital mediums, typography is a bigger beast to tame. In this talk, Devos will introduce some web typography tips about the web typeface and formatting which leads to the best reading experience.

Suzanne Scacca

A Reference Guide For Typography In Mobile Web Design
Time：13 PM - 15PM, February 1st, 2021

Description: In terms of how to handle typography in mobile web design, it appears that simpler and safer works best. In this talk, Scacca will lead to break down the elements designers need to pay attention to in mobile typography and then visit what the research says about how to handle them.
